Kalia Price is currently a third-year Psychology student at the University of California, Riverside. She is planning on graduating in Spring 2021 with a Bachelor of Arts and with Honors. Kalia Price's success at the University of California, Riverside started the day she stepped on campus as a freshman. Ms. Price was nominated to be a part of the University Honors her senior year in high school as she had above a 4.0-grade point average. Since then, Kalia has been an active member of University Honors and recently completed her capstone project, called I am more than my melanin: Kalia Price.

Kalia is also very involved in extracurricular activities on campus. She is a part of the following: Physician Assistant Tomorrow, an organization where students can learn about the Physician Assistant profession and different Physician Assistant programs, learns how to suture, and receives presentations from Doctors and Physician Assistants, and also participates in volunteering events; Project Sunshine, an organization that provides free educational, recreational, and social programs to children and families living with medical challenges; Best Buddies at UCR, an organization that gives people with disabilities to explore new friendships and widen their social circles; the National Association for the Advancement of Colored People (NAACP), where she puts on programs and events for youth to be more advanced in voting rights and political representation, economic sustainability, education, health, and juvenile justice. Some examples are Adopt-A-Family, Martin Luther King Walk, STEM Academy program for elementary to high school African American students every Saturday; Kalia currently holds the position of Vice President of the Riverside Youth Council; and lastly, she is a part of Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Incorporated, an organization of college-educated women committed to the constructive development of its members and to public service with a primary focus on the Black community. Outside of school, Kalia also is a part of Club Med, where she virtually shadows different medical professionals which include, but not limited to PA, MD, RN, NP.

Prior to her attending the University of California, Riverside, Kalia earned her certification as a medical assistant back in 2018. She completed an externship at Rainbow Pediatrics in Upland, under Doctor Steve Firme for six months, earning over two hundred ninety-two clinical hours. She currently works as a caregiver, also known as a Direct Care Staff, at Independent Options, Incorporated. Her duties include: assisting with activities of daily living: bathing, grooming, dressing, showering, etc., administering medication (Certified), cooking nutritious meals, and provides assistance with eating, basic computer skills for charting and other documentation of behaviors, seizures, and vital signs, social interaction and teaching skills to clients to increase mental and physical capabilities, assist and teach clients with the performance of Assistance of Daily Living (ADLs). While working at Independent Options, Kalia has earned over eight hundred hours of patient care experience. Kalia has previously worked at Chipotle Mexican Grill as a crew member for six months in 2018 and at Starbucks as a barista from August 2019 until November of 2020.
